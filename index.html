Top three activities to do at Kathmandu

Visit Pashupatinath Temple

A sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, Pashupatinath is one of the most revered religious sites in Nepal. The temple complex, located along the Bagmati River, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a vibrant center of spirituality, rituals, and heritage.

Visit Fewa Lake

Nestled in the heart of Pokhara, Fewa Lake is famous for its serene waters and the stunning reflection of the Annapurna Range. Visitors can enjoy boating, lakeside cafés, and the beautiful Tal Barahi Temple situated on an island in the middle of the lake.

Visit Annapurna Base Camp

A trekker’s paradise, Annapurna Base Camp offers breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks and lush valleys. The journey combines natural beauty with cultural encounters, making it one of Nepal’s most rewarding adventures.
